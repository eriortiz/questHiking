Welcome to the Great Outdoors!


Here at Quest Hiking Co., we are all about travel and enjoying the great outdoors with your friends and family in order to grow closer with the ones you love.


Our mission is to inspire people to explore the far and wide, going to the furthest corners of the Earth by bicycle, foot, or kayak, and to provide gear, from tents, to water bottles, to backpacks, to climbing equipment in order help the backpacking community thrive. Brought to you by men and women who enjoy the wilderness and the smell of a warm campfire.


We also have a large selection of hiking apperal, from sunglasses to keep the light out of your eyes, to boots to help you get through those muddy terrains, and jackets to keep you warm on those cold forest or winter nights.
